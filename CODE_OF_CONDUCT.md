# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Be respectful, direct, and technical.

We want a repository where people can discuss implementation details, bugs, trade-offs, and design boundaries without harassment or hostility.

## Unacceptable behavior

This includes:

- harassment or personal attacks
- discriminatory language
- threats or intimidation
- bad-faith disruption of discussions or reviews
- publishing private information without permission

## Maintainer responsibility

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject comments, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that violate this standard.

## Scope

This applies to issues, pull requests, discussions, and other project communication channels.
